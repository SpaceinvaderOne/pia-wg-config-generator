About PIA Server Availability

PIA VPN servers occasionally experience high load or undergo maintenance. This is normal and doesn't mean your config file is invalid. The config generation always succeeds - connection issues happen when your WireGuard client attempts to connect to the server.

What to try:

  • Wait 5-10 minutes and try the same region again
  • Generate a config for a different region (US East is typically reliable)
  • Check if other regions in the same country are available

Connection Maintenance: Once connected, the PersistentKeepalive setting (25 seconds) maintains your connection reliably, even through brief network interruptions.
